About Our Team

Alex, our Director, leads the overall operations of the company with a clear vision and a steady hand. He coordinates between departments, sets strategic goals, and ensures that projects are completed on time and to the highest standard. His ability to foresee potential challenges and address them early keeps the organization operating smoothly and effectively. Supporting him is Michelle, our Deputy Director, who plays a crucial role in managing daily functions and assisting with decision-making. She works closely with both the leadership team and employees to ensure processes are efficient and aligned with company objectives. Michelle’s approachable nature and attention to detail help maintain a collaborative and productive work environment.


On the ground, Michael, our General Manager, oversees the day-to-day operations that keep the business running. He manages schedules, monitors performance, and ensures that resources are used effectively. This foresight allowed the organization to gain a competitive edge, improve productivity, and offer services that matched the evolving needs of customers. His leadership style is inclusive, encouraging input from all levels of the organization. He firmly believes that valuable insights can come from anywhere — whether it’s a junior employee working on the front lines or a senior manager with years of experience. This openness to ideas fosters a culture of innovation and collaboration that runs throughout the company. Working alongside him is Michelle, our Deputy Director, whose role is both supportive and deeply involved in operational execution.
